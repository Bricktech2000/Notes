## notation

the [[vector-space]] of $m$ by $n$ [[matrix]]es is denoted as follows:

$\mathbb M^{m, n}$ in my [[math-notation]]

$M_{m\ n}(\mathbb R)$ in [[classical-math-notation]]

## definition

$\mathbb M^{m, n} M$, where

$M$ is a [[matrix]]
