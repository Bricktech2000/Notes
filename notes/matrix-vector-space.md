# Matrix Vector Space

see [[vector-space]], [[math-notation]], [[matrix]]

> **notation**: _in my [[math-notation]]_
>
> the [[vector-space]] of $m$ by $n$ [[matrix]]es is denoted as follows:
>
> $\mathbb M^{m, n}$

> **notation**: _in [[math-notation]]_
>
> $M_{m\ n}(\mathbb R)$

> **definition**:
>
> $\mathbb M^{m, n} M$, where
>
> - $M$ is a [[matrix]]
