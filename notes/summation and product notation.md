# Summation and Product Notation

**see** [[math notation]]

## summation notation

let $S = x_0 : \dots x_n$

then, in [[conventional math notation]],

$S = \sum_{i = 0}^{n} x_i$

## product notation

let $S = x_0 \mid \dots x_n$

then, in [[conventional math notation]],

$S = \prod_{i = 0}^{n} x_i$
