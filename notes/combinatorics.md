## Y Combinator

_allows for [[recursion]] in [[lambda-calculus]]_

_used to formally define recursive [[function]]s in a [[functional-programming]] language that does not support [[recursion]]_

$Y = \lambda f.\ (\lambda x.\ f\ (x\ x)) (\lambda x.\ f\ (x\ x))$
