# Even Number

**see** [[math notation]], [[boolean algebra]], [[number]]

> **example** **see** [[even odd proof examples]]

**definition**

$\mathbb E n =\!= \mathbb Z k \land n = 2k : 0$

**properties**

$\mathbb E = +\mathbb O$, see [[odd number]]

$\mathbb E = n \rightarrow (0 + \psi\ n\ 2)$, see [[psi function in mat2348]]

$\mathbb E < \mathbb Z$
