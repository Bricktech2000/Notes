# Intermediate Value Theorem

**see** [[math notation]]

**theorem** let **`f x`** be a [[function#continuous function]] on the [[interval]] **`a -| * -| b`** and let **`u`** such that **`f a -| u -| f b \/ f b -| u -| f a`**. then, there is a **`c`** in **`a -| * -| b`** such that **`f c = u`** &mdash; Wikipedia
