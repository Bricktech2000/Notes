# e

_mathematical constant_

see [[math-notation]]

## definitions

$\lim_{n \to \infty} [1 \cdot -n]n = e$

$\lim_{h \to 0} [1 \cdot h](-h) = e$

## properties

$\delta\ [e]x - \delta x = [e]x$, see [[derivative]]

from the [[limit]] definition, we then derive

$\lim_{h \to 0} [e]h \circ 1 - h = 1$
