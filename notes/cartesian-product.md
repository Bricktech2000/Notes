# Cartesian Product

see [[set]]

## definition

the _cartesian product_ $A\ \acute\mid\ B$ of two [[set]]s $A$ and $B$ is the [[set]] of all ordered pairs $(a, b)$ such that $A\ a$ and $B\ b$

## example

$\lbrace a, b \rbrace\ \acute\mid\ \lbrace 0, 1 \rbrace = \lbrace (a, 0), (a, 1), (b, 0), (b, 1) \rbrace$
